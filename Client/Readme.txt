
Tank Battle Contest Client Sample APP

There are 2 folders :
1.  MyTankDll
    This is Visual Studio solution for implementing your AI logic
    
2.  MyTankJNI
    This is Eclipse project for running your dll and connect to server.
    
You can use and develop your AI using above sample, OR create your own client app to connect to server at port 9021.
Please read the rule on the Tank Battle City Presentation.pdf.

Happy coding!
